How to use it

  1. Choose the pockets Joe is targeting.
  2. Joe calls a pocket before the pitch.
  3. Tap Attempt on that pocket.
  4. If he hits the called pocket, also tap Hit.
  5. Use Undo for the last action or reset when starting a new round.
